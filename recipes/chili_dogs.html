Chili Dogs

Description

Ingredients

  • Hot dogs
  • Hot dog buns
  • Ground beef
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Canned crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Chili powder
  • Optional: shredded cheese, chopped onion, ketchup, mustard

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef and onion.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, chili powder, and simmer.
  3. Grill or boil hot dogs.
  4. Assemble: hot dog in bun, chili on top, (optional) with cheese, onion, ketchup, mustard.
