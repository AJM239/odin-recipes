Chili Dogs

Description

Chili dogs are a hot dog dressed up in a savory, meaty coat. They feature a hot dog nestled in a bun, topped with a hearty chili made from ground beef, simmered with tomatoes, onions, and chili powder. This creates a delicious fusion of textures - the soft hot dog and fluffy bun contrasting with the chunky chili. You can customize them further with shredded cheese, chopped onions, ketchup, and mustard for a flavor explosion.

Ingredients

  • Hot dogs
  • Hot dog buns
  • Ground beef
  • Onion (chopped)
  • Canned crushed tomatoes
  • Tomato sauce
  • Chili powder
  • Optional: shredded cheese, chopped onion, ketchup, mustard

Steps

  1. Brown ground beef and onion.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, chili powder, and simmer.
  3. Grill or boil hot dogs.
  4. Assemble: hot dog in bun, chili on top, (optional) with cheese, onion, ketchup, mustard.
